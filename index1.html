01

Smart Banking Tools

  • Smart Banking Tools are digital technologies used to make banking faster and more convenient.
  • These tools allow customers to access banking services anytime and anywhere.
  • Mobile Banking Apps help users check balance, transfer money, and pay bills easily.
  • Internet Banking allows customers to manage their bank accounts online.
  • ATMs (Automated Teller Machines) provide cash withdrawal and basic banking services.

02

Digital Wallet

  • A Digital Wallet is an electronic tool used to store money digitally.
  • It allows users to make cashless and online payments easily.
  • Digital wallets can be used for shopping, bill payments, and recharges.
  • They store debit card, credit card, and bank account information securely.
  • Digital wallets support quick payments through QR codes.

03

Payments & Transfers

  • Payments & Transfers allow users to send and receive money digitally.
  • These services help in making quick and secure financial transactions.
  • Money can be transferred between bank accounts easily.
  • UPI enables instant payments using mobile phones.
  • Users can pay utility bills, mobile recharges, and online shopping payments.
